Name: Ascension of Christ
Artist: Dosso Dossi
Year: 16th Century


Not much information can be found about this particular painting, Ascension of Christ, however here is some information concerning it's author: Dosso Dossi. Dosso Dossi (c. 1489–1542, real name Giovanni di Niccolò de Luteri), was an Italian Renaissance painter who belonged to the School of Ferrara, painting in a style influenced by Venetian painting, in particular Giorgione and early Titian.
